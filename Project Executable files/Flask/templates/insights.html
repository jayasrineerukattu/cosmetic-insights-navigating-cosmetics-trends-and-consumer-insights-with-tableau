Cosmetics Industry Insights

The top cosmetics brands are Clinique and Shiseido, with moisturizers leading in sales. Most products—especially from Clinique—are unsuitable for sensitive, dry, and normal skin types. La Mer offers the highest-priced items across all categories. Overall, top brands and product categories maintain high ratings, averaging around 4 to 4.5 stars.
